## What Are Backlinks?

Backlinks are links from other websites that point to your website. Search engines like Google use backlinks as a strong ranking signal - they indicate that other sites find your content valuable and trustworthy.

---

## Why Backlinks Matter

1. **Search Engine Authority:** More quality backlinks = higher domain authority
2. **Better Rankings:** Pages with more backlinks tend to rank higher
3. **Referral Traffic:** Backlinks bring direct visitors to your site
4. **Trust Signals:** Quality backlinks show your business is legitimate

## Important Notes

1. **Quality Over Quantity:** 10 quality backlinks are better than 100 spam links
2. **Natural Growth:** Focus on earning links, not buying them
3. **Relevance:** Get links from automotive, local business, and service-related sites
4. **Anchor Text:** Use natural, varied anchor text (not always "Warwick Quick Tyres")
5. **Patience:** Building backlinks takes time - be consistent
